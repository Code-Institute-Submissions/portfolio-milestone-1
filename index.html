projects

FIFA World Cup 2018

EVS operator during FIFA World Cup 2018 in Russia

I had a privilege to be a part of the team that brought World Cup to the TVs in the whole world. I was based in Sochi and worked on six games that were happening there. Biggest highlight of the trip was doing replays during quarterfinal - Russia v Croatia. Game full of emotions!

Olympics

Video editor in Rio 2016 and Pyeongchang 2018

Working as video editor is not in my day to day schedule, that is why I found this experience very special. During summer Olympic Games in Rio 2016 I was cutting highlights of boxing, while in Pyeongchang 2018 I was responsible to provide edits of ice-hockey games.

Premier League

Premier League and Championship football coverage for Sky Sports

2019 has been extremely exciting - it was my first full year working on Premier League games for Sky Sports - which is the top sporting company in UK. I feel this has been my biggest career accomplishment so far.

Athletics

EVS operator during World Relays Championship in Bahamas 2017

Adding athletics to my CV skill set has been very important. To have veriaty of sports proves I am able to adapt to any situation. During this job I was in charge of providing hyper-motion replays, focusing on beauty shots.
